Simple block on one line:

<div>foo</div>

And nested without indentation:

<div> <div> <div> foo </div> <div style=">"/> </div> <div>bar</div> </div>

And with attributes:

<div> <div id="foo"> </div> </div>

<div class="inlinepage"> <div class="toggleableend"> foo </div> </div>
